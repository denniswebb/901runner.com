Kubernets Pod Logs to Elasticsearch

January 1, 2018
kubernetes logging elasticsearch fluentd jq curl

While troubleshooting why some pod logs weren’t being pushed to Elasticsearch by Fluentd on our clusters. I suspected that the format of the logs themselves were the issue. After a bit of work I came up with this solution:

kubectl logs <PODNAME> | jq -c '. | {"index": {}}, .' | curl https://<ELASTICSEARCH_ADDRESS>/<INDEX>/<TYPE>/_bulk --data-bin @-

Below is a sample response from sending the last 5 log messages from a pod:
